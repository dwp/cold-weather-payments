When are benefit systems scanned and citizens paid?

The following systems will be scanned on 17 February 2023:

  • Income Support Computer System (ISCS), for citizens on Income Support and Pension Credit
  • Jobseeker's Allowance Payment System (JSAPS), for citizens on Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA) and Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

These citizens should be paid 3 working days later on 22 February.

The Tax Credit System will be scanned on 22 February. This scan finds citizens who've got a disabled child but do not have this element included in their benefit claim. These citizens will be paid 3 working days later on 27 February.
