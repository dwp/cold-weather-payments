About this service

Overview

Use this service to check Cold Weather Payment trigger data. You can view data from:

  • today
  • the previous week, every Thursday
  • any previous day this season

The Cold Weather Payment season runs annually from 1 November to March 31.

Citizens get a £25 payment every time the mean temperature at a weather station is recorded as, or forecast to be, zero degrees celsius or below over 7 consecutive days.

Read more about Cold Weather Payments on GOV.UK.
