When are benefit systems scanned and citizens paid?

The following systems will be scanned on {{scanPeriodDates1}}:

  • Income Support Computer System (ISCS), for citizens on Income Support and Pension Credit
  • Jobseeker's Allowance Payment System (JSAPS), for citizens on Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA) and Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

These citizens should be paid 3 working days later on {{payPeriodDates1}}, unless they're eligible because they've got a child who is disabled.

Citizens who've got a child who is disabled should be paid on {{dlaPayDates1}}, 3 working days after a scan of the Child Tax Credit System on {{dlaScanDates1}}.
