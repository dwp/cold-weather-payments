When are benefit systems scanned and citizens paid?

A scan of the following systems will happen on 17 February 2023:

  • the Income Support Computer System (ISCS), for citizens on Income Support and Pension Credit
  • the Jobseeker's Allowance Payment System (JSAPS), for citizens on Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA) and Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

These citizens should be paid 3 working days later, on 22 February 2023.

A scan of the Tax Credit System will happen on 22 February 2023. This scan finds citizens who have a disabled child but do not have this element included in their benefit claim. These citizens will be paid 3 working days later, on 27 February 2023.
