If you're eligible, you get 4 Cold Weather Payments so far this season

Check if you meet the eligibility criteria to get Cold Weather Payments.

The season runs annually from 1 November to 31 March.

You get a £25 Cold Weather Payment for each of the following cold weather periods:

  • Saturday 21 January 2023 to Friday 29 January 2023 - updated to include 2 additional days of cold weather
  • Friday 13 January 2023 to Thursday 19 January 2023
  • Tuesday 3 January 2023 to Monday 9 January 2023
  • Tuesday 27 December 2022 to Monday 2 January 2023

Last updated 21 August 2023

Why you get Cold Weather Pyaments for these periods

The average temperature in your area was either recorded as, or forecast to be, zero degrees celsius or below over 7 consecutive days.

The temperature in your area is measured at [weather station name].

There are 72 Cold Weather Payment weather stations across England and Wales. The temperature at [weather station name] is the most similar to the temperature where you live.
