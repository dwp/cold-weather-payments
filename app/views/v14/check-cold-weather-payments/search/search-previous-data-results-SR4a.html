When are benefit systems scanned and citizens paid?

A scan of the following systems will happen on 16 December 2022:

  • the Income Support Computer System (ISCS), for citizens on Income Support and Pension Credit
  • the Jobseeker's Allowance Payment System (JSAPS), for citizens on Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA) and Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)

These citizens should be paid 3 working days later, on 21 December 2022.

A scan of the Tax Credit System will happen on 21 December 2022. This scan finds citizens who have a disabled child but do not have this element included in their benefit claim. These citizens will be paid 3 working days later, on 28 December 2022.
